Our Customers

We cater to clients who want our bikes. Most are passionate about biking and see it as part of a lifestyle. This might mean they are competitive, like to style their vehicles, incorporate biking into their daily lives for transportation, or may just be a weekend warrior. We are a business-to-customer organization (B2C).
